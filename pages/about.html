About The Place Gym

The Place Gym was established in 2018 with the goal of helping people become healthier, stronger, and more confident. Our modern facilities, certified trainers, and supportive fitness community have helped thousands of members achieve their goals.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower people to live healthier lives through professional fitness coaching, quality equipment, and personalized training programs.

Our Vision

To become Cambodia's most trusted fitness center by delivering outstanding customer service and promoting healthy lifestyles for everyone.
